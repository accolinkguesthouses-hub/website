Privacy Policy

Last updated: 30 April 2026. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide when creating an account (name, email address, phone number), when listing a property (property details, photos, contact information), and automatically through your use of our platform (browsing data, search queries, device information).

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To operate and improve the Accolink platform
  • To connect guests with guesthouse owners
  • To send booking notifications and service updates
  • To personalise your experience and show relevant listings
  • To ensure platform safety and prevent fraud

3. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal data. We share limited information with third parties only where necessary — for example, when you contact a guesthouse, your name may be visible to the property owner. We use Supabase for secure data storage.

4. Data Security

Your data is stored securely using industry-standard encryption. We use Supabase's Row Level Security to ensure users can only access their own data. All data transmission occurs over HTTPS.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data at any time. To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@accolinkguesthouses.com or through your account settings.

6. Cookies

We use essential cookies to keep you signed in and remember your preferences. We do not use advertising cookies or share cookie data with advertisers.

7. Contact

For privacy concerns, contact us at privacy@accolinkguesthouses.com.
