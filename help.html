For Guests

How do I find a guesthouse? ▼
Browse our listings on the home page. Use the search bar to filter by city and budget, or use the city cards to explore by destination. Click "Explore Guesthouse" on any listing to see full details, photos, amenities, and reviews.
Do I need an account to browse listings? ▼
No — you can browse all listings, photos, amenities, and reviews without an account. However, you'll need a free Accolink account to contact a guesthouse or leave a review.
How do I contact a guesthouse? ▼
Open any listing and click "WhatsApp" or "Call". WhatsApp will open with an automated message ready to send. The call button will dial the guesthouse directly. You'll need to be signed in to use these features so we can track your booking status.
How do I leave a review? ▼
Sign in, open a guesthouse listing, and scroll down to the reviews section. Select a star rating, write your experience, and click "Submit Review". You can only leave one review per property.
Can I track my bookings? ▼
Yes. Go to your Dashboard after signing in. Under "Bookings" you'll see all your pending and confirmed booking requests. Once a guesthouse confirms, your booking status changes from Pending to Successful.

For Hosts

How do I list my guesthouse? ▼
Create a free account, then click "List Property" in the navigation. Follow the 5-step form: basic info → description & contact → amenities → photos → review & publish. Your listing goes live immediately.
Is it free to list? ▼
Yes, listing your property on Accolink is completely free. We charge no commission or subscription fees. Our mission is to connect Botswana's guesthouses with travellers.
How do I see how my listing is performing? ▼
Your Dashboard shows real-time analytics for each property: total views (how many people scrolled past your listing), clicks (how many opened your listing), booking requests (how many contacted you), ratings received, pending bookings, and successful bookings.
How do booking requests work for hosts? ▼
When a guest contacts you via WhatsApp or Call, a booking is automatically logged as "Pending" in both dashboards. Once you've confirmed with the guest directly, go to your Bookings tab and click "Confirm" to mark it as Successful — or "Cancel" if unavailable.
Can I add multiple properties? ▼
Yes. You can list as many properties as you own. Each property has its own analytics, booking tracker, and reviews. Access all your listings from the "My Properties" section of your dashboard.
How do I get the Featured badge? ▼
Featured status is currently assigned by Accolink based on listing quality, photo count, review scores, and booking activity. Properties with complete profiles, great photos, and positive reviews are prioritised. Contact us to request a review.

Bookings

What does "Pending" mean? ▼
Pending means a guest has initiated contact via WhatsApp or phone, but neither party has confirmed the booking yet. Once you've spoken and agreed on dates, the host should click "Confirm" in their dashboard to mark it Successful.
What WhatsApp message gets sent? ▼
The automated message reads: "Hello! I would like to enquire about availability at your guesthouse." This is prefilled in WhatsApp so the guest can send it immediately or customise it before sending.
Does Accolink handle payments? ▼
No. Accolink facilitates introductions between guests and hosts. Payment arrangements are made directly between the two parties — cash, EFT, or any method you agree on.

Account

How do I reset my password? ▼
On the sign-in page, click "Forgot password?" and enter your email. You'll receive a reset link. If you don't see it, check your spam folder.
Can I delete my account? ▼
Yes. Email us at support@accolinkguesthouses.com with the subject "Delete My Account" and we'll remove all your data within 7 business days.
